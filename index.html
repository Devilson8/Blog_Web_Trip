Top 3 cities to visit in 2024

Here are some of the best cities to visit in 2024, based on trends, cultural events, and unique attractions:

1. Tokyo, Japan - With the completion of several major projects and the return of international events, Tokyo remains a top destination for its blend of modernity and tradition.

2. Rome, Italy - Rome’s rich history and new archaeological discoveries make it an evergreen favorite, enhanced by ongoing restorations and exhibitions.

3. Lisbon, Portugal - Known for its picturesque streets, historic architecture, and culinary delights, Lisbon continues to grow in popularity.
